# mhd-health-insurance-chart

Standalone embeddable chart showing Town of Marblehead health insurance spending from FY2002 through FY2027 (budgeted), with shaded eras for the pre-GIC escalation, the GIC switch, the long plateau, and the recent rebound.

## What it shows

- Annual town spending on health insurance, FY02–FY27
- Hover for share of general fund in each year
- Key annotations: $18M peak (FY06), GIC switch (FY07), $15.8M FY27 budget
- Callout on the levy squeeze: the FY27 increase alone (~$2.1M) consumes nearly all of the ~$2.2M annual Proposition 2½ levy growth

## Files

- `index.html` — self-contained page, loads Chart.js 4.4.1 from CDN

## Use

Open `index.html` in a browser, or embed via iframe.

## Sources

FinCom budget presentations, GIC rate announcements, and Marblehead Independent reporting. FY27 figure based on adopted GIC rates ($15,814,317).
